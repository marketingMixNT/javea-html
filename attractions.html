    Attractions

    Things to Do in Javea

    Javea is not just a destination; it’s a mosaic of experiences, each uniquely enriching. Here’s a guide to the top-rated attractions and activities in Javea, highly recommended by travelers

    Pristine Beaches and Secluded Coves

    Arenal Beach

    The crown jewel of Javea’s beaches, Arenal is famed for its soft sands, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant promenade. It’s the perfect spot for families, sunbathers, and water sports enthusiasts.

    Cala Granadella

    Tucked away in a picturesque cove, this small beach is a haven for snorkelers and nature lovers. Its tranquil waters and stunning scenery have made it a top-rated spot for a peaceful retreat.

    Historical Landmarks

    Church of San Bartolome

    This Gothic-style church stands as a testament to Javea’s rich history. Its impressive architecture and historical significance make it a must-visit for history buffs.

    Soler Blasco Archaeological and Ethnographic Museum

    Located in the old town, this museum offers insights into the area's ancient past, showcasing artifacts and exhibits that trace back Javea’s heritage.

    Natural Wonders

    Montgo Natural Park

    A paradise for hikers and nature enthusiasts, the park offers trails leading to some of the most breathtaking views in the region. The hike up Montgo Mountain is a highlight, offering a panoramic vista of the coastline.

    Cape San Antonio

    Located on a cliff offering spectacular sea views, this natural reserve is perfect for a scenic walk or a relaxing picnic.

    Water Sports and Adventures

    Jet-Skiing and Kayaking

    The clear waters of Javea are ideal for water sports. Many local operators offer jet-skiing, kayaking, and paddleboarding experiences.

    Scuba Diving in Cabo San Martín

    Explore the underwater world with guided scuba diving tours. The marine life and underwater landscapes here are simply extraordinary.

    Cultural Experiences

    Javea’s Old Town

    Wander through the narrow streets of the old town, lined with traditional houses, charming cafes, and artisan shops.

    Javea Artisan Market

    Discover local crafts, food, and art at the vibrant artisan market. It’s a great place to find unique souvenirs and gifts.

    Each of these places offers its own unique flavor of Javea’s diverse and rich tapestry, promising memorable experiences for every visitor. Whether it’s the lure of the beach, the call of the wild, or the allure of history, Javea has something special for everyone.
