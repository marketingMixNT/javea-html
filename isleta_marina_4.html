    Isleta Marina 4

    Isleta Marina

    Book Now

    Welcome to discover the charm of the ISLETA MARINA apartment, located in the picturesque town of Javea on the coast of Spain. This four-room apartment spanning 73 square meters is the perfect place for a summer vacation for families and groups of friends.

    The interior of the apartment combines classic with modernity, creating a harmonious space that captivates with attention to detail. Subdued colors such as warm browns, beiges, greens, and grays promote relaxation and rest, creating an atmosphere of elegance and tranquility.

    The living room connected to the kitchen is the heart of the apartment, where guests can spend time together preparing meals or relaxing after a day full of experiences. The living room features a comfortable three-seater sofa and an armchair, providing a cozy place to relax. A large 55-inch television provides entertainment for the whole family.

    The kitchen is fully equipped and ready to prepare delicious meals. You'll find everything you need, including an oven, induction hob, microwave, refrigerator, dishwasher, and coffee maker. A large round table for six provides a comfortable place to enjoy meals.

    The ISLETA MARINA apartment has three bedrooms, each providing comfortable and restful sleep.

    The first bedroom, Dormitorio, charms with its cozy decor and elegance. Equipped with a large double bed, it provides the perfect conditions for relaxation after a day spent by the sea. It delights with a mural depicting an exotic rainforest, creating a unique atmosphere of relaxation. Additionally, thanks to its own spacious bathroom with a luxurious shower, guests can enjoy full comfort and privacy.

    The next two bedrooms are equally charming and cozy, ideal for children, friends, or other family members. The second bedroom is equipped with two single beds, providing comfort and flexibility for guests. The third bedroom, with a fold-out double bed, provides excellent space for additional guests, also with access to a small balcony where you can enjoy a moment of peace and relaxation with a cup of coffee. A second bathroom is dedicated to guests of these rooms.

    Each bedroom is equipped with built-in wardrobes, providing ample storage space for luggage.

    From the living room and the dormitorio bedroom, we step out onto a large, sunny terrace measuring 20 square meters, where you can enjoy the fresh air and views of the surrounding landscape. The terrace is equipped with a large table with six chairs and two comfortable loungers, providing an ideal place to relax. Installed awnings guarantee protection from the sun on hot days.

    The apartment has all the amenities to ensure a comfortable stay. Air conditioning and electronically operated window blinds guarantee optimal temperature all year round. Additionally, the apartment is equipped with a washing machine and two beach loungers.

    The ISLETA MARINA apartment has access to an underground garage, where there is a designated parking space for our guests. This is an ideal solution for those traveling by car, providing not only safety but also convenience when parking.

    The ISLETA MARINA apartment delights not only with its interior but also with its spacious garden. The garden surrounding the apartment complex is an oasis of peace, a green enclave. The central point of the garden is the swimming pool - an excellent place to cool off on hot days. The pool has been designed with guests of all ages in mind, with a children's pool where the youngest can safely play under adult supervision. The pool surroundings have been carefully arranged to provide comfort and convenience, with parasols and an outdoor shower. It's the perfect place for a relaxing day outdoors, enjoying the beautiful weather and views of the surrounding landscape. The garden provides privacy and tranquility, creating a unique atmosphere where you can spend unforgettable moments with family and friends.

    The ISLETA MARINA apartment is not only about unique interiors but also an excellent location that provides easy access to the main attractions of Javea.

    It offers convenient access to beaches, making it an excellent choice for sea lovers. The sea is only 300 meters from the apartment, allowing our guests to easily reach the beach within a few minutes' walk. The proximity of Plaja Javea beach, just 700 meters away, makes it an excellent place for seaside walks and sunbathing for the whole family.

    For those seeking even more attractions, the Plaja de Arenal beach, just 850 meters from the apartment, offers more opportunities for water recreation and amenities such as restaurants, cafes, and shops, as well as a beautiful sandy beach. With such a close location to the beaches, our guests can fully enjoy the charms of the coast and spend unforgettable moments in one of the most beautiful towns in Spain - Javea.

    For lovers of maritime adventures, the port is only 1.5 kilometers from the apartment. It's the perfect place to admire picturesque views of boats and yachts.

    To experience the authentic atmosphere of Javea, we encourage you to take a walk through the old town, located 2.5 kilometers from the apartment. Narrow streets, historical buildings, and traditional Spanish restaurants create a unique atmosphere there.
