    Long Term Rental

    Overwiew

    Discover the charm of Javea throughout the year with our long-term rental options. Perfectly suited for those wishing to extend their stay between the peak seasons, our long-term rentals offer the comfort of home in the idyllic setting of Spain's Costa Blanca.

    Benefits of Long-Term Renting:

    Cost Savings

    Enjoy reduced rates compared to short-term holiday rentals, allowing for a more economical stay without compromising on the quality of living.

    Cultural Immersion

    Living in Javea for an extended period offers a unique opportunity to truly immerse yourself in Spanish culture and the local community.

    Remote Work Haven

    With high-speed internet and serene settings, our apartments are ideal for remote workers and digital nomads looking for a change of scenery or a productivity boost.

    Mild Winters

    Escape the cold and spend your winter in the mild and pleasant climate of Javea, where sunny days are abundant.

    Education Opportunities

    For families, Javea is home to reputable international schools such as Xàbia International College and Lady Elizabeth School, offering quality British education while enjoying the Mediterranean lifestyle.

    Long-Term Rental Opportunities

    Our long-term rentals include a range of properties that cater to different needs, from cozy flats for solo adventurers or couples to larger apartments that fit for families. Each property comes fully furnished with all the amenities required for comfortable living.

    Remote Work-Friendly Spaces

    Properties equipped with dedicated workspaces ensure productivity is maintained.

    Family-Friendly Environments

    Choose from rentals near bus stop to the top international schools, making it seamless to balance family life with the pursuit of education.

    Whether you're looking to relocate for work, seeking a seasonal home away from home, or wanting to give your children an international educational experience, Javea's long-term rentals offer a solution tailored to your aspirations. Explore our listings and find the perfect setting for your extended stay in this beautiful part of Spain.
